About CrimeCat

This awesome app was created for the Police hackathon in Sweden and should only be used as a demonstrator. With this app you can create a picture and annotate important parts on the picture. The image is automatically synchronized, so that you can work with multiple colleagues at the same time

No Cats were hurt in the making of this app.
